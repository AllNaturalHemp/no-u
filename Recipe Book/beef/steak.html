Steak

This here is Wayne's perfect steak.

If you want a good steak with no frills or sauce, here you go.

Ingredients

  • 1-inch thick 'berta beef top sirloin
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Directions

  1. Let sit for 10-15 minutes until room temp.
  2. Salt and pepper heavily.
  3. Grill at 400, 4 minutes total.
  4. Flip each minute to get the good grill marks.
  5. Let sit for 2 minutes.
  6. Down the hatch.
