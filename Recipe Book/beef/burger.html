Burgers

This is my recipe for the perfect burgers!

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Chopped onion
  • Shredded cheddar

Directions

  1. Combine ground beef, chopped onions, and shredded cheddar in a big bowl.
  2. Mix thoroughly.
  3. Separate into patty sized portions.
  4. Cook until desired temp is reached.
