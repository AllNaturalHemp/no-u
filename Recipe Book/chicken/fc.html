FRIED CHIMKEN

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs, wings, and drums
  • Flour
  • Pepper
  • Eggy
  • Oil (veg, peanut, or olive)

Directions

  1. Thaw chimken.
  2. Mix flour and pepper in a big bowl.
  3. Whisk eggy in seperate bowl for eggy wash.
  4. Lightly flour chimken.
  5. Dunk chimken in the eggy wash.
  6. Flour chimken again.
  7. Wash with eggy again!
  8. One more round of flour!!!!!
  9. Heat oil until nice and hot.
  10. Let the chimken swim in the oil until desired temp is reached.
  11. EAT!
